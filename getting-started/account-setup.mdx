Proton VPN uses your Proton account for authentication. If you already use Proton Mail or another Proton service, you can sign in with the same credentials.

## Sign in with an existing account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Proton VPN">
    Launch the app. If you have not signed in before, you will see the welcome screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Sign in">
    Tap **Sign in** to open the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your Proton account username (or email) and password, then tap **Sign in**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete any additional verification">
    If your account has two-factor authentication enabled, enter your TOTP code or use your security key when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a new account

If you do not have a Proton account yet, you can create one directly from the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Proton VPN">
    Launch the app and tap **Create an account** on the welcome screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a username and password">
    Pick a username for your Proton account and set a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    Proton may ask you to complete a CAPTCHA or other human verification step to protect against abuse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Choose the Free plan to get started immediately, or select a paid plan to unlock additional servers and features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish setup">
    Once your account is created, you are signed in and ready to connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Free vs paid plans

Proton VPN offers a free tier alongside paid subscriptions.

| Feature | Free | Plus |
|---------|------|------|
| Server locations | Limited selection | 90+ countries |
| Server count | Limited | Thousands of servers |
| Simultaneous connections | 1 | 10 |
| VPN protocols | WireGuard (UDP/TCP/TLS) | WireGuard (UDP/TCP/TLS) + Stealth + Smart |
| NetShield (ad & tracker blocking) | Not available | Available |
| SecureCore | Not available | Available |
| Streaming support | Not available | Available |
| Speed | Medium | Highest |

<Note>
  Free accounts always have access to at least one server in a limited set of countries. There are no time limits or data caps on the free plan.
</Note>

## Switching accounts and logging out

To sign out of your current account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the side menu or settings">
    From the home screen, tap the menu icon or navigate to **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Sign out">
    Scroll to the bottom and tap **Sign out**. The app will clear your session and return you to the welcome screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Signing out while a VPN connection is active will disconnect you from the VPN.
</Warning>

To sign in with a different account, complete the sign-out steps above and then sign in with the other account's credentials.
