Proton VPN for Android is a free, open-source VPN application built on the same privacy principles as Proton Mail. It protects your internet connection using industry-leading protocols — WireGuard, IKEv2/IPSec, and Smart protocol — and includes advanced features like NetShield ad blocking, SecureCore routing, and split tunneling.

The app is available on Google Play, F-Droid, and GitHub. All builds (except F-Droid) are signed with the same certificate, and the source code is fully auditable under the GPLv3 license.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Proton VPN">
    Download the app from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ch.protonvpn.android), [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/ch.protonvpn.android), or [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ProtonVPN/android-app/releases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to your Proton account">
    Open the app and log in with your Proton account credentials. No account yet? Tap **Create account** to sign up for free — no email address or personal information required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to VPN">
    Tap the **Connect** button on the home screen to connect to the fastest available server automatically. You can also browse the server list to pick a specific country or city.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your protection">
    Enable NetShield to block ads and trackers, turn on the Kill Switch to prevent data leaks, or configure split tunneling to choose which apps use the VPN.
  </Step>
</Steps>
